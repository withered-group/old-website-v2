Project Requirements

If you're unsure if you meet a requirement, please contact us

Here's everything you'll need to comply with to get into Withered Hub:

These are not Terms of Service, however failing to comply with them may result in you being rejected/kicked from Withered Hub and/or your account's termination

Your project must be of a supported type
You must have project info (title, description, category, icon, etc.) ready
You must have a publicly-ready build of your project
You must not share any information you are given without explicit permission
Your project must not be uploaded to Withered with malicious intent (damage to our servers or other projects, etc.)
Your project must only contain your assets or assets that you and Withered are legally allowed to use
